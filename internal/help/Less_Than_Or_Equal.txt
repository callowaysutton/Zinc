The '''&lt;=''' condition symbol denotes that a value must be less than or equal to another value for the condition to be True. 


''Example usage:'' IF x [[Less_Than_Or_Equal|&lt;=]] 320 THEN PRINT &quot;Left or center of screen&quot;
